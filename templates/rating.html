Customer ratings work on a rating between 1 to 5, 5 being very positive experience, 1 being a negative experience. When organized by rating, the applied clustering does present some patterns.

Please see the graph below to see how this data is organized. The graph is interactable, click on any of the inner circles to focus on a specific slice. Hovering the mouse over a slice will also pull up additional information.

In regards to customer experience, Some revealing examples in regards to clusters:

Positive Review Sentiment

Top cluster: 7

7: hotel, stay, room, great, service, staff, location, place, restaurant, breakfast

Negative Review Sentiment

Top cluster: 3

3: smell, room, dirty, hotel, carpet, stay, sheet, stain, Chelsea, annex
