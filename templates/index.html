TripAdvisor is a travel planning website that specializes in hosting extensive information for accomodations and amenities for anyone planning a trip. The site also hosts user-generated reviews for those accomodations, providing reviews and feedback. For this project, hotel reviews were aggregated to provide bulk analysis regarding the language used. The purpose of gathering and analyzing these reviews is to observe any patterns or common sentiments among the language used when examined in aggregate.

Techniques Used

Natural Language Processing, usually shortened as NLP, is a branch of artificial intelligence that deals with the interaction between computers and humans using the natural language. The ultimate objective of NLP is to read, decipher, understand, and make sense of the human languages in a manner that is valuable.

A number of different processes are applied to the text before machine analysis can be applied.


K-means Clustering is a method of vector quantization that aims to partition observations into a number of clusters in which each observation belongs to the cluster with the nearest mean. For this project, the processed text is analysed to see how well words associate with other words.
